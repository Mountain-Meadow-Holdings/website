About

Who We Are

Mountain Meadow Holdings is a privately held company founded on the belief that meaningful wealth is built through patient, principled ownership — not short-term transactions. We acquire, launch, and advise businesses with the intent to hold them indefinitely, developing a portfolio of cash-generating assets across industries.

We are operators first. Our approach emphasizes hands-on management, disciplined deal selection, and long-term value creation over exit-driven returns.

Our Focus

What We Do

Our activities are organized across three complementary arms, each aligned with a distinct strategy for building and sustaining long-term value.

Acquisition Arm

Acquisitions

We source and acquire established, profitable small businesses with strong cash flow. Our model is buy-and-hold — we take companies under long-term management with no intent to sell, building a growing portfolio of durable, real-economy assets.

Venture Arm

Ventures

We found and capitalize new companies, maintaining meaningful equity stakes through the holding company. These are calculated, high-conviction bets — businesses we believe in and are willing to build from the ground up.

Service Arm

Advisory

We provide strategic advisory services to early-stage companies and their investors, engaging in exchange for retainer and equity. This arm keeps us close to emerging opportunities and connected to the broader investment community.
